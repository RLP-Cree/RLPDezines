Beautifying the World

My creative process is an act of service. When I design, I am channeling prayer into form—creating symbols of resilience, health, and happiness that you can carry with you.

Whether it's a tumbler, a blanket, or a tote, these everyday items are vessels for that energy. This isn't about volume; it's about making a way to share a good vibe, a good prayer, and a more beautiful perspective with anyone who needs it.

A Prayer in Every Design

I did not start this just to create merchandise. I started this to contribute to the well-being of our community as a cultural pillar. The geometry and colors in my work come directly from my own personal beadwork, my Grass Dance regalia, and teachings I've carried for over 35 years.

I create these designs from a place of spiritual alignment, drawing them up to share a little piece of who I am with the world.

RLP Dezines is an expression of that journey. My hope is that anyone can wear these items as a badge of spiritual, mental, emotional, and psychological strength.

The Mission

To offer an act of service to the Indigenous spirit. Through prayerful art and traditional geometry, I aim to provide everyday items that serve as a reminder of our collective strength and connection to the Creator's cosmos.

The Vision

To see these designs carried as a reflection of health, happiness, and cultural pride. My vision is for our creative work to serve as a bridge, ensuring that the light and beautiful colors of our heritage continue to shine through.

"Let the colors shine. It’s going to make the world a more beautiful place."

— Ronnie Lodgepole, Founder

Store Policies & FAQ

Online Only / Storefront

RLP Dezines operates 100% online. I don't have a physical brick-and-mortar storefront right now—everything is run directly through this site so I can ship my custom drops directly to your door anywhere in Indian Country. Town of Gilbert Business License: BUSLIC001339-05-2024

Crafted Specifically For You (Final Sale)

Every item is crafted specifically for you at the time of purchase as a vessel for these designs. Because of this custom, print-on-demand model, I do not accept returns, exchanges, or offer cash refunds for buyer's remorse or incorrect size selections. Please review all product descriptions and size charts carefully before completing your purchase.

Damaged or Defective Items

I stand behind the quality of my designs and materials. In the rare event that your item arrives misprinted or defective, contact me at rlp@rlpdezines.com within 7 days. I will make it right with a free replacement.

Shipping & Taxes

Shipping costs and applicable taxes are calculated automatically by Square at checkout based on your exact shipping address. Once shipped, you will automatically receive a tracking number via email.
