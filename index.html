Our Story

Photo by Aaron Tsosie | Indigenous Event Recordings

What is an Indigipreneur?

An Indigipreneur is an Indigenous person who shares their resilience to create a meaningful life and clear a path for the next generation. It is a modern expression of the ingenuity that has always been in our DNA.

By utilizing modern tools and technology, we operate from our own perspective. Whether in design, education, or service, we see the world through an Indigenous lens, grounding our work in the values we were raised with.

It is the merging of the entrepreneurial spirit with the Indigenous spirit.

Art Born From Experience

I did not start this just to create designs. I started this to contribute to the well-being of our community. I have walked through my own share of challenges and seen the struggles our people face.

I found my way through our traditional ways. Through prayer, the sweat lodge, the discipline of training, the power of laughter, and the energy of the dance arena, I found healing. I learned how to channel that energy into something positive for my family and my community.

RLP Dezines is an expression of that journey. My hope is to share the strength and healing I have found with you.

These designs are meant to be a companion for your day—whether on apparel, blankets, or everyday items that keep you connected to your roots.

The Mission

To offer a space of encouragement for the Indigenous spirit. Through the synergy of our traditional values and modern design, I aim to provide items that serve as a reminder of our collective strength.

The Vision

To see RLP Dezines worn as a reflection of health, happiness, and cultural pride across Indian Country.

"In a timeline full of noise, we are focusing on what truly matters."
