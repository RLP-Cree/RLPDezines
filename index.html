The Ethos

Unapologetic & Indigenous

RLP Dezines is rooted in my identity as a Chippewa Cree from Rocky Boy, Montana. This isn't just apparel; it’s a statement. My perspective is informed by ancestral roots spanning from northern Alberta down to Crow country, translated for the modern world.

We are taking traditional geometry, floral patterns, and cultural touchstones out of the archives and putting them onto the streets. Every piece is built for a new era of Indigenous representation, resilience, and resistance.

If you know my satirical parody pages, you know the vibe. We don't do quiet. We don't do passive. This brand is about taking up space, flexing our survival, and owning our narrative with a strong, unapologetic attitude.

"My goal is to create gear that serves as a badge of strength, survival, and undeniable style for our community."

— Ronnie Lodgepole, Founder

Store Policies & FAQ

Online Only / Storefront

RLP Dezines operates 100% online. I don't have a physical brick-and-mortar storefront right now—everything is run directly through this site so I can ship my custom drops directly to your door anywhere in Indian Country. Town of Gilbert Business License: BUSLIC001339-05-2024

Crafted Specifically For You (Final Sale)

Every item is crafted specifically for you at the time of purchase as a vessel for these designs. Because of this custom, print-on-demand model, I do not accept returns, exchanges, or offer cash refunds for buyer's remorse or incorrect size selections. Please review all product descriptions and size charts carefully before completing your purchase.

Damaged or Defective Items

I stand behind the quality of my designs and materials. In the rare event that your item arrives misprinted or defective, contact me at rlp@rlpdezines.com within 7 days. I will make it right with a free replacement.

Production & Delivery Times

Once an order is paid for, it goes directly into production. Your item will be printed within 3 to 5 business days, undergo a quality check, and then ship out. Because this is a custom print-on-demand process, it doesn’t arrive overnight. You can expect your gear delivered to your door within 2 weeks (typically much quicker, but this allows for shipping traffic and availability).

Shipping & Taxes

Shipping costs and applicable taxes are calculated automatically by Square at checkout based on your exact shipping address. Bundle pricing reflects community bulk rates for events and specials. Once shipped, you will automatically receive a tracking number via email.
