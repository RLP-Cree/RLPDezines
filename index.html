The Founder

Ronnie
Lodgepole

I grew up around the fireplace, surrounded by ceremonies from the time I was young. That upbringing informed every part of my worldview — it taught me how to channel creative energy to pay blessings forward.

I found my way through our traditional ways. Through prayer, the sweat lodge, the discipline of training, the power of laughter, and the energy of the dance arena — I found healing. I learned how to channel that into something positive for my family and our community.

RLP Dezines is an expression of that journey. The music is the soul. The designs are the outward voice.

"Creator blessed my mind to be creative, and this is how I share it with the world."

— Ronnie Lodgepole, Founder
